The BCFRUIT4YOU company was founded by 3 students who are passionate about providing local fruits for those rainy weekends in the lower mainland, a farmer’s market on the web. You can shop through local fresh produce while sitting on your sofa with a cup of coffee in hand. Your favorite fruits will be packed and ready to be picked up at a warehouse.

BCFRUIT4YOU deals with independent growers and producers who take pride in their quality and freshness. They provide us with high quality fruit and produce at very reasonable prices, which we can pass onto you.

We keep customers updated via email, Facebook, and Twitter as the produce becomes available all summer long. Just sign up for our weekly newsletter so we can send you prices and availability for all our fresh picked produce. You pre-order through the online shop, then collect the produce at the designated pick up locations and pay, with cash or cheque, at time of pick up. It is that easy.

Please share our website with your family and friends. Our business relies 100% on referrals so if you like what you get let others know.
