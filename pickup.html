Pick Up Locations

Please read the important information below, along with our pick up stops and times. We take great pride in bringing the Fraser Valley and Okanagen goodness to a location near you. We will try to carry some extra produce on our trips but we cannot promise availability unless you pre-order through our online store.

Our order goes to our farmers on Monday and the truck leaves friday morning. The farmers go out and pick the produce we want Friday and we pick it up and deliver it to you immediately. So fresh, it’s like you picked it yourself. Of course, we always order extra but sometimes that produce goes quickly.
